
KPI'S Requirement:

Total Revenue: The sume of the total prize of all pizza orders
Average order value: The avg amount spent per order, calculated by dividing the total revenue by the total number of orders.
Total pizza sold: The sum of Quantities of all pizza sold.
Total Orders: The total number of orders placed.
Average pizzas per order: The avg number of pizza sold per order, calaculated by dividing the total number of pizzas sold by the total number of orders.
